# Policy - PII Handling and Data Privacy

## Definition of PII
Personally Identifiable Information (PII) includes data that can identify an individual directly or indirectly, such as:
- Full name, national ID number, phone number, address, bank account number, email.

## Principles
- Data minimization: collect only what is necessary.
- Masking: display sensitive data partially (e.g., **1234).
- No OTP/PIN: it is prohibited to request or store OTP/PIN.
- Auditability: access and changes must bu logged for accountability.

## Retention
- Operational logs are retained only as needed and according to the retention policy.
- Sensitive data must not be stored in application logs.
