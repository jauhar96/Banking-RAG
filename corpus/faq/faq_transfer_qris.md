# FAQ - Transfers and QRIS

## Interbank transfers
- Interbank transfers can be performed via the Transfer menu.
- Always verify the destination account number before confirming.

## QRIS
- QRIS payments are completed by scanning a QR code using the in-app camera.
- If a transaction shows as pending, wait a few minutes and re-check the transaction history.

## Fees and limits
- Fees and limits may vary depending on transfer type and applicable bank policies.
- The current limit is typically displayed on the transaction confirmation screen.
