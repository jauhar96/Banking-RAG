# FAQ - Login and Security

## Login failure
- Ensure your internet connection is stable.
- Couble-check that your username/password is correct.
- If you are logging in from a new device, additional verification may be required.

## Reset PIN/Password
- Use the in-app help/support menu to reset your credentials.
- Never share your OTP with anyone, including parties claiming to be bank staff.

## Lost device
- Immediately request account access blocking through the support channel.
- Identify verification is required before account recovery.
